# Product — NeighborCircle

NeighborCircle is a senior-focused social connection platform designed to reduce loneliness and provide emotional and practical support.

## Target Users
- Older adults (low technical confidence)
- Volunteers offering companionship or help

## Core Features
- Dashboard with reminders and emotional check-ins
- Volunteer matching system
- Community interaction page
- Friend matching system

## Product Goals
- Reduce social isolation
- Encourage meaningful human interaction
- Provide emotional reassurance and support
- Build a sense of belonging

## Design Philosophy
- Warm, welcoming, and human-centered
- Simple and easy to navigate
- Emotionally supportive and reassuring

## UI Expectations (IMPORTANT)
- Large readable fonts
- Warm colors (yellow, orange, soft red)
- High contrast text
- Large buttons and simple layouts
- Friendly, non-technical language
